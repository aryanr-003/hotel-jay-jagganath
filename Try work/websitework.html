About Us

A Legacy of
Warm Hospitality

Hotel Jay Jagannath has been a trusted name in hospitality for over a decade. We pride ourselves on delivering genuine comfort, hearty meals, and a welcoming environment that feels like home — rooted in the spirit of Lord Jagannath's boundless grace and generosity.

Whether you are checking in for the night, or celebrating a special occasion, our team is devoted to making every moment count. Come as a guest, leave as family.

The Room +
Our well-appointed 240 sq. ft. rooms are thoughtfully designed for comfort and convenience. Each room features an attached private bathroom and modern amenities including air conditioning, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi, comfortable bedding, a flat-screen television, wardrobe space, and essential toiletries. Whether you're travelling for business or leisure, our rooms provide everything needed for a relaxing stay.
The Reception +
Enjoy seamless hospitality with our 24/7 reception service. Our friendly and professional staff are always available to assist with check-ins, check-outs, travel guidance, local recommendations, and any guest requirements. We are committed to ensuring a smooth and comfortable stay at any hour of the day or night.
The Lobby +
Our spacious and hygienic lobby offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere for every guest. Designed with comfort and cleanliness in mind, it provides the perfect space to relax, meet friends, or unwind after a long day. With well-maintained surroundings and comfortable seating, every visit begins with a pleasant experience.
